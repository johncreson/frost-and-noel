## Scene List (7 Scenes)

### Scene 1: Ice Rink Setup (Noel POV)
**Location:** Town ice rink, morning
**Goal:** Establish setting; Noel already overworking
**Conflict:** Day 7 exhaustion catching up
**Exit shift:** Eben notices; Noel dismisses

**Key beats:**
- Beautiful winter day; rink decorated
- Noel has been up since 4 AM
- Eben watches him with concern
- "You should rest." "After the event."
- *They've had this conversation before.*
- This time it lands differently.

---

### Scene 2: The Skating Event (Dual)
**Location:** Ice rink
**Goal:** Fun, community, hidden exhaustion
**Conflict:** Noel performing competence while struggling
**Exit shift:** The mask is slipping

**Key beats:**
- Families skating; kids laughing; perfect holiday scene
- Noel works the crowd, charming as always
- Eben handles logistics from the edge
- Hugh: "You should skate together! For photos!"
- Noel stumbles—recovers fast, but Eben sees.
- Their eyes meet across the ice.
- *He knows.*

---

### Scene 3: The Fall (Noel POV)
**Location:** Ice rink
**Goal:** Noel gets injured; catalyst for caretaking
**Conflict:** Noel's body gives out; he has to accept help
**Exit shift:** Can't pretend anymore

**Key beats:**
- Helping a kid learn to skate
- Noel's tired legs give out at the wrong moment
- He falls—hard. Wrist hits the ice.
- Pain, sharp and real
- For a second, he can't get up
- Then Eben is there. Immediately.
- "Don't move."
- Noel: "I'm fine—"
- Eben: "You're bleeding."
- *He wasn't fine.*

---

### Scene 4: The Care (Eben POV)
**Location:** First aid station / quiet room
**Goal:** Eben takes care of Noel completely
**Conflict:** Noel doesn't know how to receive this
**Exit shift:** He starts learning

**Key beats:**
- Eben walks Noel off the ice
- First aid: wrapped wrist (sprain, not break)
- Eben cleans the cut on Noel's palm
- Gentle. Focused. Quiet.
- Noel tries to joke. Eben doesn't let him deflect.
- "You don't have to be fine."
- Noel goes quiet.
- Internal (Eben): *Let me take care of you. Please.*

---

### Scene 5: The Quiet Space (Noel POV)
**Location:** Back room / private
**Goal:** Noel confronts his fear of being cared for
**Conflict:** Wound activated: "I'm only worth keeping when useful"
**Exit shift:** Starting to believe otherwise

**Key beats:**
- Alone with Eben in a quiet space
- Noel's hand wrapped; he feels useless
- Internal: *What am I supposed to do? Just sit here?*
- Eben: "Rest."
- Noel: "I can't just—"
- Eben: "You can. I've got the event."
- Noel stares at him
- "You're volunteering to handle everything. So I can rest."
- Eben: "Yes."
- *Noel's eyes sting.*

---

### Scene 6: The Almost (Dual)
**Location:** Garland Cup, evening
**Goal:** Noel almost says "I need you"
**Conflict:** The words are there; he can't quite say them
**Exit shift:** Almost-confession hangs in the air

**Key beats:**
- After the event; cocoa at Garland Cup
- Noel's wrist wrapped; Eben sitting close
- Quiet. Intimate.
- Noel: "Today you..."
- Eben waits
- Noel: "I don't know how to... I've never..."
- Eben: patient. "Take your time."
- Noel: "I need—"
- He stops. Swallows.
- *He can't finish the sentence.*
- Eben doesn't push. Just covers Noel's good hand with his.

---

### Scene 7: Hook Ending (Eben POV)
**Location:** Noel's door
**Goal:** Set up EP8 domesticity
**Conflict:** Eben wants to stay; Noel wants to ask
**Exit shift:** Neither says it, but both know

**Key beats:**
- Walking Noel home
- At his door: "You should rest." "I know."
- Pause.
- Noel: "You could... stay."
- Eben goes still.
- "I mean. If you wanted."
- Eben's throat tight
- "I want."
- *They go inside together.*

**Final line hook:**
For the first time, Eben didn't plan his exit. He just stayed.
