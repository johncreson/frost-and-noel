### Long Blurb (150 words)
Noel Merrick has one job: save the Twelve Days to Christmas festival before his sleepless, overcommitted body gives out. He doesn't need help. He doesn't need a cold-eyed logistics expert critiquing his spreadsheets. And he definitely doesn't need Eben Frost looking at him like he's the most urgent problem in the room.

Eben Frost has one rule: don't get attached. But Noel Merrick is chaos in a sweater, and Eben can't seem to stop putting furniture in his path and whispering "sit" like it's a commandment.

Twelve days. Twelve setpieces. One relentlessly festive small town determined to marry them off.

They agree this is temporary.
They're both liars.
