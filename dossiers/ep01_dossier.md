## Episode Logline

When Tinsel Town's holiday festival threatens to implode on opening day, overworked event director Noel Merrick is assigned a cold, competent fixer he didn't ask for: Eben Frost. Neither expects the friction to feel like fire.

---

## Character States (Start of Episode)

### Noel Merrick
- **Status:** Festival event director, day 1 of 12-day nightmare
- **Emotional state:** Stressed, overextended, running on caffeine and denial
- **Wound active:** "I'm only lovable when I'm useful"
- **Wants:** To prove he can handle this alone
- **Needs:** Someone to share the weight

### Eben Frost  
- **Status:** Hired by Mayor Kringle as logistics fixer (Noel doesn't know he's coming)
- **Emotional state:** Controlled, assessing, armored
- **Wound dormant:** "Attachment destroys what I build"
- **Wants:** Clean execution, then exit
- **Needs:** Something to disrupt his isolation

## Plot Summary

1. Festival kickoff day - multiple crises hit simultaneously
2. Eben arrives as "help" Noel didn't request
3. Immediate friction: Noel resents being managed, Eben evaluates everything
4. They're forced to work together on the chaos
5. Eben sees Noel's exhaustion; Noel sees Eben's competence
6. By evening, they've saved day 1 as a team
7. They agree to continue "temporarily"
