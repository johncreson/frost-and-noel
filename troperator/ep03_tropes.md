## Chapter 1: Avoidance Dance

**Primary Trope (Engine):** THE ELEPHANT IN THE ROOM
- Forces action: Must maintain normalcy around the undiscussed
- Reshapes landscape: Everything is about the kiss by not being about the kiss
- Visible consequences: Hyperawareness of each other

**Secondary Trope (Complication):** THE BETRAYING BODY
- Sharpens cost: Eyes keep finding him; hands remember
- Intensifies primary: Can't will yourself into not-wanting

**Atmospheric Trope (Mood):** THE FORCED CHEER
- Acts against characters: Festival demands smiles while they're dying inside
- Somatic details: Brightness feeling harsh, sounds too loud
- Complicates decisions: No quiet space to process

**Somatic Anchors:**
- Lips still feeling the press
- Tracking his position always
- The ache of deliberate distance

**POV Alignment:** Fear + Geometry (constant awareness)
