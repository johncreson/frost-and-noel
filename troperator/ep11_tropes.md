## Chapter 1: Christmas Eve Morning

**Primary Trope (Engine):** THE DECISION TO FIGHT
- Forces action: Noel chooses courage over protection
- Reshapes landscape: He's not giving up
- Visible consequences: Getting up, going out, being brave

**Secondary Trope (Complication):** THE LAST CHANCE (ticking clock)
- Sharpens cost: Today or never
- Intensifies primary: Urgency drives action

**Atmospheric Trope (Mood):** THE CHRISTMAS EVE HOPE
- Acts against characters: Holiday pressure to resolve
- Somatic details: Morning cold, holiday sounds, the weight of today
- Complicates decisions: Everything heightened by the day

**Somatic Anchors:**
- Getting dressed with intention
- The decision in the mirror
- Today is the day

**POV Alignment:** Courage (chosen) + Fear (present but overridden)
