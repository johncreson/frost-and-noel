## Chapter 1: The Collapse

**Primary Trope (Engine):** THE CRISIS (physical)
- Forces action: Market collapsed; must respond
- Reshapes landscape: Everything planned is ruined
- Visible consequences: Disaster that could break them

**Secondary Trope (Complication):** THE GROWTH MOMENT
- Sharpens cost: Noel asks for help (new behavior)
- Intensifies primary: This is character growth in action

**Atmospheric Trope (Mood):** THE DEVASTATION
- Acts against characters: All their work, destroyed
- Somatic details: Collapsed tent, scattered goods, the cold
- Complicates decisions: Fix it or surrender?

**Somatic Anchors:**
- The ruined tent
- "I need everyone" (growth)
- Eben already beside him

**POV Alignment:** Leadership + Vulnerability (asking for help)
