## Chapter 1: Ice Rink Setup

**Primary Trope (Engine):** THE OVERWORK PATTERN
- Forces action: Noel pushing past limits
- Reshapes landscape: Establishing the fall's setup
- Visible consequences: Physical exhaustion visible

**Secondary Trope (Complication):** THE CONCERNED OBSERVER
- Sharpens cost: Eben watching, worried, not yet intervening
- Intensifies primary: The tension of waiting for the break

**Atmospheric Trope (Mood):** THE HOLIDAY HUSTLE
- Acts against characters: No time to rest, always more to do
- Somatic details: Cold air, setup noise, Noel's tired bones
- Complicates decisions: Work or self-care?

**Somatic Anchors:**
- The fourth coffee of the morning
- Eyes that burn
- Hands on autopilot

**POV Alignment:** Necessity (must work) + Denial (of exhaustion)
