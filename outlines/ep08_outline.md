## Chapter 1: The Collapse
**POV:** Noel | **Word Target:** ~1,400

### Opening Hook
*"The holiday market collapsed. The actual, physical holiday market. Because the universe had a sense of comedic timing that Noel did not appreciate."*

### Scene Content
- **Setting:** Holiday Market site, morning
- **The disaster:** Main tent collapsed overnight

### Beats to Hit
1. Arriving to devastation—tent down, booths ruined
2. Weather damage overnight
3. Vendors panicking. Mayor Kringle in full spiral.
4. **Noel's first instinct: "I need everyone."**
5. Growth moment: he asks for help

### Character Growth
*"A month ago, Noel would have tried to rebuild the entire thing by himself using spite and caffeine. That had been his pattern. Fix it. Don't ask. Be useful."*

*"'I need everyone,' he said instead. And meant it."*

### Eben's Response

- *He asked. Didn't take over.*
- "Everyone. I need everyone."

### Chapter Hook
The call goes out.

## Chapter 4: 3 AM Moment
**POV:** Eben | **Word Target:** ~1,300

- **The realization:** Domestic intimacy at its peak

### The Quiet
1. Market 90% done
2. Noel asleep on a stack of tarps, wrapped in Eben's coat

4. The night feels suspended

### Internal
